CHRIS HEMSWORTH #THOR

CHRISTOPHER HEMSWORTH(BORN ON 11 AUGUST 1983)IS AN AUSTRALIAN ACTOR.HE ROSE TO PROMINENCE PLAYING KIM HYDE IN THE AUSTRALIAN TELEVISION SERIES HOME AND AWAY(2004-2007) BEFOR BEGIINING A FILM CAREER IN HOLLYWOOD. IN THE MARVEL CINEMATIC UNIVERSE(MCU),HEMSWORTH STARTED PLAYING THOR WITH THE 2011 FILM OF THE SAME NAME AND MOST RECENTLY REPRISED THE ROLE IN THOR:LOVE AND THUNDER(2022),WHICH ESTABLSIHED HIM AMONG THE WORLD'S HIGHEST PAID ACTORS
